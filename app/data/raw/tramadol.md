Title: Tramadol Use in Nigeria

Tramadol is a pain relief medication that is often misused in Nigeria, especially among young adults.

Common reasons people misuse tramadol include:
- Coping with emotional pain
- Increasing physical endurance
- Peer pressure

Risks of tramadol misuse:
- Addiction and dependence
- Seizures, especially at high doses
- Breathing problems
- Mental health issues such as anxiety and depression

Signs of tramadol dependence may include:
- Needing higher amounts to feel the same effect
- Feeling sick when not using
- Difficulty stopping even when it causes harm

Reducing harm:
- Avoid mixing tramadol with alcohol or other drugs
- Seek medical help before stopping suddenly
- Talk to a trusted health professional if use feels out of control

Help in Nigeria:
- Federal Neuro-Psychiatric Hospitals
- Teaching hospitals
- NGOs offering addiction support
